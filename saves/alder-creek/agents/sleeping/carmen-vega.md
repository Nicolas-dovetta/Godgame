# Carmen Vega, 36 — SLEEPING (returned home Day 171; story resolved Day 185)

**Why sleeping:** came to Alder Creek Day 170 with her son Mateo (7) on a
ventilator two states away; the god sent her home with "there is nothing here
for you" and made her loud about it — her debunking video ended the pilgrim
flood. Four days later a doctor caught a thought that wasn't hers (allergies;
a fragrance); hypersensitivity pneumonitis diagnosed; Mateo off the
ventilator Day 182, asking for waffles Day 185.

**Return hook:** the letter pinned inside Sam's till ("mark mine paid...
the loudness was part of it, wasn't it"). She knows what happened and agreed,
unasked, to keep the secret. If the town ever needs a fierce, credible
outside voice — or if Mateo (a boy who owes his breath to a bank shot) ever
travels to see a certain chair — the Vegas come back.

**Personality:** direct, unsentimental, does the math even when the math
frightens her. Keeps accounts. Pays debts.

**Relationships:** Jonah (left the porch light on for her; she noticed);
Sam (keeper of the letter); Dr. Priya Okonkwo (the fellow who "arrived" at
the answer — Carmen sends her a card every year now, which the doctor finds
inexplicably moving).
