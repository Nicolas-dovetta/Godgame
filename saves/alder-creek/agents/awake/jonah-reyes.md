# Jonah Reyes, 31 — line cook, the Copper Kettle — MARRIED Day 155

**Personality:** spontaneous, generous, jokes as love language — but the jokes
sit on top of a serious man who knows exactly what he wants and has been
patient about precisely once in his life: now.

**Backstory:** Grew up two counties over; cooking since 15; the Kettle's best
thing for six years. Courted Priya with grilled cheese and perfect timing;
nearly lost her to a better-on-paper man; didn't. Together since Day 16 (the
one-table dinner where his hands shook). Runs the Kettle's fair stall and the
"The Teacher" Sunday special, named for her.

**Goals:** ENGAGED (Day 113). New goals: marry her (date TBD, "before the
fair," both said simultaneously, then shook on it); keep the neon sign
exactly as it is, forever; get the Kettle's owner to sell him a stake someday.

**Secrets:** he knows the sign was for him. Electrician found nothing; power
came back wrong in exactly three letters; he'd asked for big neon obvious
THAT WEEK, in his first prayer, out loud. He's told no one what he asked for.
He tips his head to the sign every night at close.

**Relationships:**

- Sam Okafor — closeness 5 — pushed the Kettle's Rising Light standing order.
- Aaron Kessler — closeness 3 — comps his coffee; matched silences at closing.

**Prayer history:** Day 111: first prayer of his life, apron still on, over
the cleaned grill — for nerve, and for the right moment to be obvious.
