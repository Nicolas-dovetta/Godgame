than she's been in a year — teachers noticed.

for her dad to come home. This month it worked: David apologized, came to the
Autumn Fair (she won HIM the duck), got a transfer, and moved into the blue
house by the library — 900 meters from her bedroom. **Walkie-talkie channel 4
is in range now.** Mild asthma (Dr. Vasquez). In Priya's class.
