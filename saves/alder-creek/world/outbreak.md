hungry, bite = infection. Afflicted are fast then clumsy, never tire, never
stop. Began a metro ~1,100 miles west; outran the truth. Comms fully dead

overt horror; but the VEIL still concerns whether the GOD can be proven,
not whether the world is strange.

  Kessler steel) built overnight Day 693; two bridges barricaded; the

  family's bitten teenage son. The prep window (Days 681-694) exists to
  change how that moment lands — barricade up or not, doctors warned or not,
  town governed or panicking. Nothing is fixed yet.
