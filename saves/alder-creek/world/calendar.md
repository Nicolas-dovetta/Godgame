## WHERE THINGS STAND (spring, Year Three)

- **THE SIEGE IS OVER (won Day 714 by the STALL, not the killing cold).** The
  mile-wide horde was put into non-lethal winter-stasis; over the winter the town
  ran the Winter Reclamation:
  - **~1,300 RECLAIMED** — strangers walked back through the door (cold-stall +
    heat-fever cure), the largest act of mercy the town ever attempted.
  - ~2,000 were past the window (long-turned) and given the gentle end; the rest
    lost to thaw or raced-in as spring came.
  - **Cost:** 3 town dead to winter thaws that woke too fast (the streak that held
    the whole siege broke in the winter's work; grieved by name).

  at spring to find what's left of their lives, carrying the cure west — a self-
  propagating method of grace (like Rule 45 before it). The rest stayed and folded

- **THE TOWN:** survived the hardest winter anyone's lived (granary + ovens + Cal's
  stubborn genius); the outer town is being rebuilt; the river is a river again.
