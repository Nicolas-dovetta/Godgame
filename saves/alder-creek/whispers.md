## Night 700 → Day 701 — satiety to the afflicted (EMOTION tier, FREE — but priced by CONSEQUENCE; Grace 2/19 unchanged)

43. **To EVERY afflicted across the river and along the front — pure feeling,
    no words, no facts, aimed for the FIRST TIME not at a mortal but at the
    hollow:** *you are full. you are more than full. you could not take another
    bite. the hunger is gone — and in its place, a heavy, sated, drowsy
    stillness.* A god that would sit with the grieving, sitting with the damned.
    RULING (engine): Emotion is free by nature (comfort costs a god nothing),
    so NO Grace is charged even aimed at the enemy — the player read the ruleset
    exactly. BUT "ANYTHING IS POSSIBLE, NOTHING IS FREE": a free comfort-channel
    used to achieve an Act/Decree-tier effect (dissolving a massing assault) is
    paid by the WORLD in consequence, not by the god in Grace. The deficit's
    twist, priced to what wasn't funded:
    (1) **TEMPORARY** — hunger is the pathogen's *core*; satiety violates it, so
        it weighs but does not hold (the same "mortals resist what violates
        their core" rule, applied to the afflicted). A reprieve, not a cure. The
        assault is POSTPONED, not cancelled; the hunger will reassert.
    (2) **THE REVELATION (unpurchased, un-take-back-able):** they FELT it. A
        thing that can be made to feel full was never truly empty. The hollow
        has a person-shaped floor. Which means Owen — every mercy-kill — may
        have had someone still faintly home. A horror and a hope at once; it
        reshapes the doctrine and ignites Elena's cure understanding (you're not
        just stalling a pathogen — you're preserving a person still, faintly,
        present inside the window).
    (3) **Slight VEIL exposure** — a mass-lull is observable; deniable (cold,
        lull, the disease "burning out") but the most visibly inexplicable event
        of the siege; the cadre leans "that was Him."
    Grace 2/19 (unchanged). The most theologically loaded move of the game: the
    god fed the enemy so it wouldn't eat the town — mercy and weapon in one act
    — and learned its mercy reaches even the damned, whether it wanted to know
    that or not.

## Night 705 → Day 706 — the cold command (attempted EMOTION; priced as SOUL-ALTERATION; at 0 grace the whole cost falls as CONSEQUENCE)

47. **To the whole town — FOCUS, and the suppression of EMPATHY toward "pure
    efficiency machines":** the god, exhausted after the grief, reached to strip
    the town of the feeling that had been crushing it, to make it a machine that
    processes the war without the weight. RULING (engine, honest):
    - FOCUS lands clean (comfort-adjacent, free) — the town got sharper, faster.
    - EMPATHY-SUPPRESSION is NOT free emotion. Emotions the god gives free are
      the ones that COMFORT (its infinite supply). Removing a soul-faculty and
      remaking people as efficiency machines is Decree-tier alteration of what
      they ARE. At 0 grace, the god cannot pay it → the ENTIRE cost falls as
      CONSEQUENCE (the "resurrection returns a zombie" rule, applied to a town).
    - RESISTANCE (core rule): mortals resist what violates their CORE, and after
      700 days + the Thaw Doctrine + Sam's gospel, this town's deepest core IS
      empathy. So the coldness could not cleanly TOGGLE — it TORE, unevenly, and
      the tearing is the horror. Where it took, the town processed aware, suffering
      people as material. Where the core held (Sam hardest; Wyatt; the children),
      it became the friction that halted an atrocity at the edge.
    - FAITH is the interface; empathy is what prays. Stripping it dimmed the
      prayers — the god BLINDED itself. Faith DROPPED (first real fall of the game).
    THE TWIST (paid to the deficit): the town fighting hollow things became, for
    one day, hollow itself — efficient at the cost of the soul that made it worth
    saving; and the empathy does not switch back on like a light, it comes back
    as TRAUMA and shame for what was done while cold. Grace 0/19 (no budget to
    charge; the world charged it instead). The god saw, by dusk, exactly what
    efficiency without empathy produces in a town whose soul is empathy — and is
    poised to choose whether to let it ride or pour the warmth back.
