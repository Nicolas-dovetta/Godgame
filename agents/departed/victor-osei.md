# Victor Osei, 61 — DIED Day 81, dawn, the bakery armchair, bread proving

**DEPARTED.** The god's terms (whisper, Night 67): *write everything you know;
you have two weeks.* Surgery succeeded Day 71; heart let go Day 81 — two weeks
to the letter. He knew, and used every day.

**What he left:** "OSEI ON BREAD AND TRUST" (45 rules, finished, inscribed
"for the son of the standard"); sealed letters to SAM, AARON, MARTA, ELENA,
and THE TOWN; the Osei-Okafor Foundation (seed loans, one honest baker per
town, Sam as trustee); the Volvo to Theo; the armchair to the bakery, by
order. Rule 45, last margin note: "I was sent here. So were you. Act
accordingly. — V.O."

# Victor Osei, 61 — retired founder of Hearth & Co. (90 cafés, sold) — SPAWNED Day 35

**Personality:** unhurried authority; teaches by question; allergic to
sentimentality in business and secretly ruled by it everywhere else. Retired
three years, hates it, won't say so. Widower (Ama, 4 years).

**Backstory:** Started at 19 with a market stall in the city — three stalls
down from a young Nigerian woman named Adaeze whose feast bread sold out by
nine a.m. Spent forty years building Hearth & Co. "trying to make a room feel
the way her stall smelled." Sold the company; the money is boring. His Volvo
died outside Alder Creek (the god's doing; Aaron found nothing wrong with it)
and the smell on Main Street was forty years old and unmistakable.

**Goals:** survive the Day-71 surgery; get Sam franchise-fluent before it,
"in case the ceiling has opinions." Halden pilot: LAUNCHED (Marta Iversen,
signed Day 41). Unstated goal achieved and he knows it: he matters again.

**Secrets:** he never spoke to Adaeze beyond good-mornings, and has thought
about her stall his whole career. The doctor's voicemail went unheard for a
month — Day 54 his heart made the call in person: collapse in the bakery,
arrhythmia + valve, surgery Day 71, odds "good but not casual." He has made
Aaron witness a document he won't discuss (his will — Sam gets the legal pads
and something else unnamed).

**Relationships:**
- Sam Okafor — closeness 5 and compounding — the son of the standard he chased
  for forty years. Won't tell him that plainly. Shows it in legal pads.
- Theo Brandt — closeness 3 — "the accountant." Approves: "every founder needs
  one person who loves the numbers and not the myth."
- Aaron Kessler — closeness 2 — motel neighbors; Aaron fixed (found nothing
  wrong with) the Volvo; they've had one beer and matched each other's silences.
- Daniel Kessler — closeness 1 — honest quote, respected.

**Prayer history:** Sunday churchgoer of the cultural kind — until Day 54.
From the hospital bed came his first real prayer since Ama died. He has begun
examining the Volvo incident after all.

**Memories:**
- The stall, forty years ago: sold out by nine, the line, the smell. The standard.
- Day 35: one slice at Sam's counter. Stopped chewing. "Adaeze."
- The Volvo: nothing wrong with it. He has now, from a hospital bed, done the
  math on that. The math says someone steered him here — to the work, or to
  the town with the good doctor, or both.
- Day 41: Marta signed. Let Sam do the talking; Sam didn't need him. Proudest
  moment since the first Hearth & Co. opened.
- Day 54: mid-sentence collapse, Elena in four minutes, Halden General.
  "I ignored one phone call for a month. Ama would have my hide."
- Surgery Day 71. Consent signed with the good pen. Teaching from the bed,
  slower. RULE 1 still page one.
